About Us

At KPG Roofings, we specialize in providing high-quality imported roofing tiles tailored to your needs. With decades of experience and a commitment to excellence, we help you create the home of your dreams while ensuring durability and style.

At KPG Roofings, we believe in delivering not just products but exceptional service. As a subsidiary of the renowned KP Group, we leverage our extensive expertise across various industries to offer roofing solutions that stand the test of time. With over 30 showrooms nationwide, our dedicated team is always ready to assist you in selecting the perfect roofing tiles for your project, ensuring your complete satisfaction from consultation to installation.
